MY ANIME LIST

A world beyond imagination

Camille V. Umpad

CAMS

Your Name

Your Name is a 2016 animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. The story centers around two high school students, Taki and Mitsuha, who inexplicably begin to swap bodies. Set in both Tokyo and a rural town, the film explores themes of identity and connection as the characters navigate their intertwined lives.

Suzume

Suzume is a 2022 Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. The film follows 17-year-old high school girl Suzume Iwato and young stranger Souta Munakata, who team up to prevent a series of disasters across Japan by sealing doors from the colossal, supernatural worm that causes earthquakes after being released.

Weathering with You

Weathering with you is a Japanese animated film that tells the story of a runaway high schooler named Hodaka Morishima who arrives in Tokyo during a period of relentless rain and encounters Hina Amano, an orphaned girl with the unique ability to manipulate the weather, allowing her to summon sunshine through prayer; together they navigate the challenges of their lives while utilizing Hina's power to bring clear skies for people in need, leading to a complex and poignant romance set against the backdrop of a city struggling with extreme weather conditions.

Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter is a Japanese manga and anime series that follows the story of Gon Freecss, a young boy who sets out on a journey to become a "Hunter" - a licensed individual with the ability to explore and exploit the unknown - in order to find his father, a renowned Hunter who left him when he was young.

One Punch Man

One Punch Man describes a story about Saitama, a superhero so powerful that he can defeat any enemy with a single punch, leading to a life of boredom as he searches for a worthy opponent in a world full of other heroes and villains; essentially, his overwhelming strength removes any challenge from his battles, causing him to feel unfulfilled despite being a hero.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a manga and anime series that follows Yuji Itadori, a seemingly ordinary high schooler who gains powerful abilities after consuming a cursed talisman - the finger of a demon named Sukuna, becoming the host to this powerful curse; he then joins a secret organization of Jujutsu Sorcerers to hunt down and destroy the remaining fingers of Sukuna, battling various dangerous "Curses" (evil spirits born from human negative emotions) while navigating his new life as a sorcerer and the responsibilities that come with it.

Tomie

Tomie from Junji Ito's horror manga is a character depicted as a beautiful young woman with a distinctive mole under her left eye, who possesses the uncanny ability to regenerate from any injury, essentially making her immortal; her mere presence drives men to extreme obsession and violence, often leading them to kill her, only for her to come back to life and repeat the cycle, creating a terrifying and disturbing narrative around her unsettling nature.

Death Note

Death Note" is a Japanese manga and anime series where a high school student named Light Yagami discovers a supernatural notebook called the "Death Note," which allows him to kill anyone by simply writing their name in it, leading to a complex story about his moral descent as he attempts to create a crime-free world while being pursued by a mysterious detective known as "L.".

Uzumaki

Uzumaki by Junji Ito is a horror manga where a small town becomes cursed by a mysterious spiral pattern, causing the inhabitants to undergo grotesque physical transformations and experience psychological torment, with the spiral motif representing a destructive, inescapable force that slowly consumes everything in its path; essentially, it's a story about the terrifying implications of an obsessive, all-encompassing pattern, considered one of Ito's most acclaimed works due to its disturbing body horror and psychological depth.
